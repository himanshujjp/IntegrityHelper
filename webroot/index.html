Integrity Helper

Manage your integrity-related Magisk modules

Important: This tool automatically downloads and flashes modules through your root manager (Magisk/KernelSU/APatch). A reboot may be required for changes to take effect.
How it works:
  1. Click "Install" to automatically download and flash the module
  2. If an update is available, click "Update" to get the latest version
  3. Click "Install All" to install/update all modules at once
  4. Reboot your device for changes to take effect
